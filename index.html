What is FAC?

Founders & Coders (FAC) is a peer-led, tuition-free coding school based in London (since 2014) and Nazareth (beginning Spring 2017). FAC transforms talented individuals from a variety of backgrounds into sought-after software developers.
The programme begins with a 16-week immersion in fullstack JavaScript, Node.js, and Git. Working in pairs and teams, students use agile development practices and deliver projects to clients such as early-stage startups and NGOs.
